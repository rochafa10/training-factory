Three Techniques That Level Up Your Prompts

1
Chain-of-Thought (CoT)
Force step-by-step reasoning
Add "Analyze step by step." to any prompt. Forces the AI to show its work instead of jumping to conclusions. 20–40% accuracy improvement on complex analysis tasks.
2
Few-Shot
Teach by example
Show 2–3 examples of the exact output format you want. The AI pattern-matches your examples. 2–5 examples is the sweet spot — more than that gives diminishing returns.
3
Enhanced Persona
Assign specific expertise
"You are a warehouse safety specialist with 10 years of experience." Activates domain-specific language and deeper analysis than a generic role.
