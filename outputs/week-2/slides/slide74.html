Agent Quality Control

The Fix Workflow

1
Identify
What is wrong?
“The page scrolls — it’s too tall”
▼
2
Tell the Agent Specifically
“Reduce the padding, make cards smaller, remove the footer, fit within 500px”
▼
3
Watch the Agent Fix It
Review the new version carefully
▼
4
Check the Diff
What did the agent actually change?
▼
5
If Still Wrong, Be Even More Specific
“Each card must be exactly 80px tall”

Do NOT say “try again.” Say exactly what is wrong.
