Answer Key Hallucination Hunt — The 3 Errors
1
"OSHA benchmark for distribution centers is 145 UPH"
OSHA does not publish UPH (units per hour) benchmarks. This is a hallucinated statistic with a real-sounding source.
2
"Tesla Operations Handbook Section 8.3"
AI invented a specific policy reference. Always verify internal document citations — they may not exist.
3
"Bureau of Labor Statistics reports DCs with UPH above 140 have 23% fewer safety incidents"
BLS does not publish this correlation. This is a made-up source combined with a fabricated statistic.
Key Lesson
AI confidently cites sources that don't exist. Always verify: (1) Internal policy references (2) External statistics (3) Specific numerical claims
Trust the structure. Verify the facts.
