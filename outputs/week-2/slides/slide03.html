Three Upgrades to Your Prompts

Chain-of-Thought
Force step-by-step reasoning. Number your analysis steps. AI shows its work.
Best for: Analysis, root cause, planning
Few-Shot
Show 2-3 examples of the output you want. AI replicates your pattern.
Best for: Reports, handoffs, comms
Enhanced Persona
Add years of experience + "known for" traits. More specific = better output.
Best for: Emails, analysis, docs
Week 1: ROLE + CONTEXT + TASK + FORMAT
Week 2: ENHANCED ROLE + CONTEXT + STRUCTURED TASK (CoT) + FORMAT (examples)
