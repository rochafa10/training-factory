Five Things to Remember from Today

1
CoT + Few-Shot + Persona upgrade your prompts from good to great — always add “step by step” for complex analysis
2
Agents DO work, chat SUGGESTS work — that is the fundamental difference
3
MCP tools give agents new skills — like installing apps on a phone
4
Check the output, not the count — an agent that says “Done!” may have cut corners. Always verify the actual work.
5
Specific instructions + right-sized tasks — break big jobs into small batches with checkpoints
