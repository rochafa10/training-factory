Getting AI to Output in Consistent Formats

📊
Tables
"Present this as a table with columns for [X], [Y], [Z]"
Best for: comparisons, data summaries, schedules
✅
Checklists
"Create a checklist with checkboxes for each step"
Best for: SOPs, audit prep, onboarding
📝
Formatted Reports
"Write an executive summary with: headline, 3 key findings, recommendation"
Best for: leadership updates, weekly reports
📄
Markdown
"Format using Markdown with headers, bold, and bullet points"
Best for: documentation, knowledge base articles
Specify FORMAT explicitly — don't leave it to the AI to guess your preferred structure
