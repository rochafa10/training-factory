Technique 3

Persona-Based Prompting

What It Is
Assign AI a specific expert role with detailed characteristics to shape style, tone, and content focus.
✗ Basic Role
"You are an operations manager."
✓ Enhanced Role
"You are a senior distribution center manager with 15 years of experience in high-volume fulfillment. You're known for clear, data-driven communication and practical recommendations."
Make It Effective
Add years of experience
Specify expertise area
Include personality traits
Describe what they're "known for"
Match persona to audience
