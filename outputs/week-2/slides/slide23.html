Build Your Prompt Playbook

20 minutes
Your Task
Organize your prompts into categories (daily/weekly/monthly)
For each prompt: add a title, when to use, full template
Version your best prompts: note what changed and why
Identify 2-3 prompts to share with a peer AGM
Save to OneNote, email draft, or shared doc
Name Prompts Clearly
Prompt 3 → Weekly KPI Narrative - CoT
Labor thing → Shift Planning Analysis v2
Playbook Tips
Save where you'll actually find it
Include the technique used (CoT, Few-Shot)
Track which prompts save the most time
Share across DCs — your solution may help others
Update prompts as you learn what works
