Chat vs Agent: The Key Difference

Chat (Bottle Rocket)
You say"Write me a report"
AI doesWrites text in chat
OutputText you copy-paste
StepsOne response
AutonomyNone
Agent (Copilot Agent Mode)
You say"Build me a tool"
AI doesCreates files, runs commands
OutputWorking files on your PC
StepsMulti-step with approval
AutonomyHigh (you approve)
Analogy: Chat = asking a colleague for advice. Agent = delegating a task to a capable assistant.
