# 👥 Managing People 🤖 Managing Agents
1 Check the work at each station → Check the output at each step
2 Break projects into milestones → Break tasks into batches
3 Quality gate before shipping → Validate before moving on
4 Give clear instructions, not vague goals → Write specific prompts, not vague asks
5 Redirect when off track → Course-correct when output drifts
6 Standard procedures for consistency → Saved prompts for repeatability
Key insight: Your leadership experience is your biggest advantage. Managing agents is managing work -- you're already an expert.
