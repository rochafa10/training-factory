Root-Cause Debugging Framework
When an AI agent goes wrong, follow these 5 steps to fix it
1
NOTICE
Something doesn't look right in the output. Trust your instinct.
Observe
2
INVESTIGATE
Ask the agent: "Show me what you did and why you did it."
Gather data
3
DIAGNOSE
Was it a bad prompt, wrong context, missing data, or tool limitation?
Find cause
4
FIX
Rewrite the prompt, add missing context, or try a different approach.
Take action
5
PREVENT
Save the working prompt. Add it to your playbook so it doesn't happen again.
Systematize
