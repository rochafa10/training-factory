The Iteration Cycle
Great results come from refining, not from the first attempt
🔄
Continuous
Improvement
1
Build
Give the agent a clear prompt and let it generate output
2
Review
Read the full output carefully before reacting
3
Identify Issues
Note what's wrong, missing, or could be better
4
Give Specific Feedback
Tell the agent exactly what to change and why
5
Agent Fixes
The agent revises based on your precise instructions
6
Review Again
Check improvements and decide: ship it or iterate again
💡
Pro tip: Most AGMs get great results by the 2nd or 3rd iteration. Each round of specific feedback gets you 80% closer to perfect.
