Week 2 Key Takeaways

1
Chain-of-Thought
Forces step-by-step reasoning. Improves complex analysis by 20-40%. Use for root cause and multi-factor problems.
2
Few-Shot Examples
2-5 examples teach AI your exact format. Quality beats quantity. Perfect for standardized outputs.
3
Data Analysis
AI turns raw spreadsheets into executive insights in minutes. Paste, upload, or describe your data.
4
Structured Outputs
Specify FORMAT explicitly: tables, checklists, reports. The AI formats, you verify the content.
5
Prompt Library
Organize by use case. Name clearly. Version and share. Your library is your competitive edge.
6
Model Selection
Claude for writing, Gemini for speed/scale, Grok for math/real-time. Match model to task.
Target this week: 20-30 min/day savings | Build your library to 10+ prompts
