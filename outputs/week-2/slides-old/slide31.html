Three Types of Power Automate Flows

RPA
Desktop Flows
Automate mouse clicks, keyboard input, and screen actions on your local machine
Best for: Legacy systems, data extraction, repetitive desktop tasks
Triggered Workflows
Cloud Flows
Automated workflows triggered by events, schedules, or manual buttons
Best for: Scheduled reports, email notifications, data sync
Approval Chains
Process Flows
Multi-step approval workflows with routing and escalation logic
Best for: Multi-step reviews, purchase approvals, escalations
