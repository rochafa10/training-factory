Scenario
Your DC missed its productivity target by 8% last week. Leadership wants to know why and what to do about it.
Monday: 95% of target (normal)
Tuesday: 92% (2 call-outs)
Wednesday: 78% (conveyor down 3 hrs)
Thursday: 88% (running behind)
Friday: 85% (catching up)
Your Task
Use CoT to analyze this productivity shortfall
Include the data in your CONTEXT
Use "First..., Then..., Finally..." structure
Get specific recommendations, not generic advice
Prompt Structure Hint
ROLE: Operations analyst... CONTEXT: [Paste the data above] TASK: Analyze step by step: 1. First, calculate each day's impact 2. Then, identify the biggest factor 3. Next, trace the root cause 4. Finally, recommend actions FORMAT: Numbered sections...
