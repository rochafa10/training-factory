Technique 1

Chain-of-Thought Prompting

What It Is
Ask AI to show its reasoning step-by-step before giving an answer. Forces structured thinking on complex problems.
20-40%
Improvement on complex reasoning
Trigger Phrases
"Let's think step by step"
"Walk me through your reasoning"
"First..., Then..., Finally..."
Example: Operations Analysis
Analyze our overtime increase step by step: 1. First, list possible causes 2. Then, evaluate each against our data 3. Finally, identify root causes and recommend actions
