Exercise 6

Iteration Techniques

What’s Wrong Follow-Up Prompt to Type
Too long “Shorten this to under 100 words. Keep the 3 most important points.”
Too short “Expand the section on [topic]. Add specific examples.”
Too formal “Rewrite in a more direct, conversational tone.”
Too casual “Make this more professional. This is going to the regional director.”
Missing section “Add a section at the end with 3 specific next steps and owners.”
Wrong format “Convert from paragraphs to bullet-point summary. Group by category.”
Too generic “Make this specific to a Tesla DC processing 3,000+ orders/day.”
Unverifiable claims “Remove statistics you can’t verify. Replace with [INSERT ACTUAL METRIC].”
Pro Tip
Don’t rewrite the entire prompt — just tell the AI what to fix. Iteration is the skill that separates good AI users from great ones.
