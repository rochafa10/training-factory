Tier 3: Public Data

Publicly available — safe to use with any AI tool
Business Data
●Public Information
Published Tesla corporate info (job announcements), published financial information, public presentations
●Marketing Materials
Published marketing materials or brochures
●Press & Media
Press releases, blogs, social media posts
●Product Specs
Megapack product specifications
●Regulatory Filings
Filings subject to public disclosure such as SEC filings
●Public Presentations
Conference talks, publicly shared decks
This is the only tier where external AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) could theoretically be used — but Tesla policy still prohibits them. Use Bottle Rocket for everything.
Source: Tesla Data Classification & Handling Policy
