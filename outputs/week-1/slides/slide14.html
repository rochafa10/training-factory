Before vs. After: The Formula in Action

BEFORE Vague Prompt 
Help me with standup prep
AFTER Structured Prompt 
ROLE: You are an experienced operations
manager at a distribution center.

CONTEXT: I manage the morning shift at
the Tampa DC. Yesterday we processed
2,847 orders with 42 associates. We had
a conveyor issue from 2-4 PM.

TASK: Create a brief standup summary
highlighting yesterday's performance
and today's priorities.

FORMAT: Use bullet points. Under 150
words. Include:
1) Yesterday's key metrics
2) Main issue and resolution
3) Today's focus areas
