The Quality Scorecard - 5 Dimensions

1
Accuracy
Are the facts, numbers, and logic correct?
Score: 1-5
2
Completeness
Does it cover everything you asked for?
Score: 1-5
3
Format
Is it structured the way you need it?
Score: 1-5
4
Speed
Did it save significant time vs. manual?
Score: 1-5
5
Usability
Can you use it as-is, or does it need heavy editing?
Score: 1-5
Scoring Guide
25/25 Ready to use immediately
20-24 Minor edits needed (sweet spot)
15-19 Significant editing - refine your prompt
Below 15 Re-prompt or switch approach
Rate every important output on these five dimensions.
