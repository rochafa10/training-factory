Three Types of Power Automate Flows

Desktop Flows (RPA)
Automate mouse clicks, keyboard input, and legacy desktop apps
Use Cases
Data extraction from desktop apps, form filling, screenshot capture
Cloud Flows
Triggered workflows that connect cloud services automatically
Use Cases
Scheduled emails, approval chains, data sync between apps
Process Flows
Multi-step approval and routing workflows with business logic
Use Cases
Purchase approvals, vacation requests, incident escalation
