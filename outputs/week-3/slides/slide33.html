Upload and Query Demo

Watch how grounding AI in data changes the response
1
Without Data
Ask Bottle Rocket:
"What were the top 3 issues at my DC last week?"
Result:
AI says it doesn't have that info OR makes something up with generic issues that sound plausible
2
With Data
Upload CSV, then ask same question:
"What were the top 3 issues at my DC last week?"
Result:
AI references specific data points from your file: "Based on your issues log, the top 3 were..."
Observation Checklist
Ungrounded response was vague or invented
Grounded response cited specific data
AI mentioned file name or data source
Numbers matched actual file contents
Always provide data when you want accurate, specific answers.
