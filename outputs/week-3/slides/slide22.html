Why AI "Makes Things Up"

  • 1 AI learned patterns from billions of pages during training
  • 2 It predicts what words should come next — generates, doesn't look up
  • 3 No memory of sources — generates text that sounds right
  • 4 When it doesn't have real data, it fills gaps with plausible-sounding fiction
  • 5 This is called HALLUCINATION
📚 Analogy

Like someone who read every operations textbook but never set foot in a DC. They can write convincing reports, but the specific numbers are made up.

AI's goal is text that sounds right. It doesn't know the difference between real and plausible.
