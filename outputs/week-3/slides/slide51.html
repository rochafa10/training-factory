Email Errors (4)
1. Hallucinated Statistic
"Industry average 99.7%" - no source provided, likely made up
2. Math Contradiction
98.2% described as "above" 99.7% - that's actually below
3. Fake Citation
"2025 OSHA Report" - citation may not exist, verify before using
4. Hallucinated Numbers
All specific numbers (47, 12.3%, 3) - hallucinated without data provided
Analysis Errors (3)
1. Misinterpreted Trend
"Improved significantly" ignores 3 weeks of DECLINE (13.2 > 12.8 > 12.4)
2. Over-confident Causation
"Confirms" causation from conveyor - correlation does not equal causation
3. Fabricated Projection
"$47,000 savings" - precise dollar figure with no supporting analysis
Report Errors (3)
1. Fake File References
"staffing-data-2025.csv" may not exist - verify files are real
2. Fabricated Source
"NWA 2025 Annual Report" - verify this document exists before trusting
3. Over-precise Predictions
"22% overtime reduction, 18% UPLH" - too precise without supporting data
