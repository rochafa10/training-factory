Three Types of AI Mistakes

H
Hallucination
Making up facts
"Your DC processed 15,247 orders" (no data provided)
"According to the 2025 OSHA report..." (doesn't exist)
Spot It
Did you give AI this data?
D
Drift
Starting right, then wandering
Asked for template, also reorganized your folders
Asked for simple list, got complex dashboard
Spot It
Did you ask for this?
O
Over-Confidence
Presenting guesses as facts
"This will reduce overtime by 30%"
"The best practice is always..."
Spot It
Does it sound too certain?
Trust but Verify: AI output is a first draft, not a final answer.
