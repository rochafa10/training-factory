The Solution: Ground AI in Real Data (RAG)

❌ Without RAG — AI Guesses
  • 1You ask a question
  • 2AI generates from patterns only
  • 3Answer may contain hallucinated facts
Example
"Your DC processed 15,247 orders" (no data provided)
✅ With RAG — AI Searches First
  • 1You ask a question
  • 2AI searches your documents first
  • 3AI finds relevant information
  • 4AI generates answer based on what it found
  • 5Answer is grounded in reality
📂 RAG = Retrieval-Augmented Generation. Give AI a filing cabinet to search before it answers.
