Framework Quick Reference

CAR
Challenge → Action → Result
Best for problem-solving questions.
“What’s the biggest challenge you’ve faced?”
Tip: Lead with the challenge to hook the interviewer, then show your solution.
PAR
Problem → Action → Result
Best for “tell me about a time” questions. Simpler than STAR when the task is obvious.
Tip: Use when the situation and task are the same thing — no need to separate them.
SOAR
Situation → Obstacle → Action → Result
Best for adversity questions. Emphasizes what stood in your way.
Tip: The obstacle is the star — it shows resilience and problem-solving under pressure.
