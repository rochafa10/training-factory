Action: Build a Mechanism, Not Just a Fix

“Good intentions never work, you need good mechanisms to make anything happen.” — Jeff Bezos
TOOL
The structural thing you built: a process, dashboard, checklist, or meeting cadence
OWNERSHIP
A specific person accountable for the mechanism after you leave
ADOPTION
Broad implementation — not just you using it. Teams, sites, the organization.
INSPECTION
Regular auditing and course correction: metrics, reviews, continuous improvement

The interviewer hears: “This person builds systems, not heroics.”
