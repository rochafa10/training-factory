STAR — The Gold Standard for Behavioral Questions

S Situation Set the scene briefly. Where were you? What was happening?
T Task What was YOUR specific responsibility?
A Action What did YOU do? Not the team — YOU. Be specific.
R Result What happened? Quantify. What did you learn?
Use when: “Tell me about a time when...” or “Give me an example of...”  |  Target length: 2–3 minutes
