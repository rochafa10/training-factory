Result: Lead with the Headline

Don’t build up — lead with impact, then prove it (Pyramid Principle)

HEADLINE
The single most impressive outcome: “Throughput increased 22% in 6 weeks”
EVIDENCE
2–3 supporting data points that prove the headline
SO-WHAT
Why it mattered to the business: dollars saved, model adopted, recognition earned

✗ Anti-pattern: “Eventually things got better and we hit our targets.” (buries the lead)
