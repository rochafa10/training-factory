The Strengths Framework: Strength → Evidence → Relevance

Step 1
Name the Strength
Be specific, not generic.
“Operational efficiency”
not “hard worker”
Step 2
Provide Evidence
A concrete example
that proves it.
Use numbers.
Step 3
Connect to Role
Why this strength matters
for THIS position.
Show alignment.

Keep it to 60 seconds. Don’t list 5 strengths — go deep on one.

Pick strategically. Choose a strength that directly maps to a key requirement of the role.
