Band-Aid vs. Mechanism

Band-Aid (Deliver Only)

“I added 3 associates to the pack line during peak hours.”

Problem recurs when you leave or staffing changes.

Shows effort, but not leadership depth.

Solves once — doesn’t last

Mechanism (Deliver + Teach + Scale)

Deliver: “I restructured the pack station staffing model based on hourly volume data.”

Teach: “I trained 2 shift leads to run the daily capacity review themselves.”

Scale: “I partnered with Greenville and Tampa DCs to pilot the model across 3 sites.”

Solved, sustained, and multiplied
