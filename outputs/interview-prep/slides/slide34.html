Red Flags & Green Flags in an Employer

Green Flags

  • Clear expectations for the role from day one
  • Defined growth path and development support
  • Team stability — low turnover, tenured managers
  • Honest about current challenges
  • Interviewer is engaged and asks follow-ups

Red Flags

  • Vague answers about the actual role and responsibilities
  • High turnover they can’t explain
  • Rushing the interview process
  • Avoiding questions about culture or team dynamics
  • The role has been open for a suspiciously long time

Trust your gut: If something feels off in the interview, it’s usually worse on the inside.
