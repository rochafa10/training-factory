ACE Breakdown — Each Step in Detail

A
Answer
Direct response. No preamble, no throat-clearing, no “That’s a great question.” Get to the point.
Tip: Start with “Yes” / “No” / “My approach is...”
C
Cite
One specific story. Name the situation, what you did, the outcome. Quantify whenever possible.
Tip: Use numbers — “reduced by 22%” beats “improved significantly”
E
Explain
Link it back. Why does this matter for THIS role? Show you understand what they need.
Tip: “That’s the same approach I’d bring to this [role]...”
